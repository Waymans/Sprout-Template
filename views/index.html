Who is this for?

Anyone really, but here's a few ideas.

Innovators

A solid boilerplate for your project. Present your ideas with no hassle.

Engineers

A website for your designs. Easily document and share your research.

Entrepreneurs

Don't worry about how to start an online presence. We have that covered.

Blend easily with other software

We strive to give you the minimum amount of boilerplate possible, yet enough to fit a variety of needs. Using mostly HTML, CSS and JavaScript, you have the opportunity to mix with other libraries and frameworks of your choosing. You can remove unneeded pages for your projects or add a plethora of others.

Simple and elegant styling

Sprout gives you clean looking designs on every page keeping up with the latest design trends and best practices, all the while keeping our codebase neat and precise. We also don't forget about the mobile users; every page is fully responsive with them in our thoughts!

Primed and ready to go

With Sprout everything can be up and running in minutes. With just a few small changes—depending on what you need—you'll be able to share your project to anyone. Feel free to just use the front end pages or use the full package that includes an Express server with authorization and database setups.
